🏆 Our Heritage

A Proud Member of ALAA

A proud member of the Australian Labradoodle Club of America, it is our goal here at Swinging Gate to provide the most loving environment possible for our puppies.

Our multi-generational Australian Labradoodles are bred from parents out of Tegan Park and/or Rutland Manor Research Centers in Australia. Staying true to the vision of those who founded this incredible breed is an important element of our breeding philosophy.
